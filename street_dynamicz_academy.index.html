Welcome to Street Dynamicz Academy

About Us

Street Dynamicz Academy is a premier institution dedicated to teaching and promoting urban dance forms. Our academy offers top-notch training in various street dance styles, including hip-hop, breakdancing, popping, and locking.

Our passionate instructors have years of experience in the world of street dance and are committed to helping students of all skill levels achieve their dance goals.

I am a street dancer by day and a data analyst by night. I love to get people to move and get into the moment to really feel who they are and be present. I am passionate to assist people to have a healthy and wealthy life through movements.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the joy of street dance, foster creativity, and build a strong, supportive community of dancers. We believe that dance is not just a physical activity but also a means of self-expression and empowerment.
